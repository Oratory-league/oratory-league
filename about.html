About Us

Mission Statement

The Oratory League is dedicated to empowering young African voices through the art of debate and public speaking. Our mission is to foster critical thinking, eloquence, and leadership in youth, enabling them to address pressing issues and inspire change across the continent. We are committed to representing Botswana with pride, contributing to an Africa where youth voices lead with insight, empathy, and courage.

Our Values

Together, we aim to shape the future of Africa—one powerful word at a time.

  • Unity: We believe in the power of collective voices and diverse perspectives to foster a stronger, more connected Africa. We stand united in our goal to inspire collaboration and mutual respect.
  • Critical Thinking: At the heart of our mission is the drive to think deeply, question boldly, and debate respectfully. We encourage rigorous analysis of ideas and strive to build well-rounded perspectives.
  • Cultural Connection: Embracing our African heritage, we celebrate cultural diversity and encourage dialogue that bridges communities, languages, and traditions. Our journey is a testament to the strength found in understanding and honouring each other’s roots.
  • Resilience: We face every challenge with determination, believing that adversity only strengthens our resolve to stand for our beliefs and uplift each other.
  • Eloquence and Integrity: We value clarity and honesty, using words to enlighten, inspire, and connect. Every speech, debate, and engagement is an opportunity to embody integrity and purpose.

Why Sponsor Us?

By sponsoring the Oratory League, you are investing directly in the next generation of African leaders. Your support empowers passionate young voices to champion critical thought, unity, and cultural pride across the continent. Through your sponsorship, you enable these students to develop the skills, confidence, and experience they need to lead Africa into a future shaped by eloquence, empathy, and powerful ideas. Together, we can inspire a generation that stands ready to drive meaningful change, bridging communities and elevating African stories on a global stage.

Sponsorship Tiers

  • Bronze Sponsor: Pledge that covers registration fees for two students – P1 400.
  • Silver Sponsor: Covers registration fees for the whole team – P7 000.
  • Gold Sponsor: Covers most fees – registration fees and food, or registration fees and accommodation for the whole league – P13 000 or P19 000.
  • Platinum Sponsor: Full sponsor package, supporting the entire league with registration fees, accommodation and food – P25 000.

Sponsorship of the Oratory League offers more than just financial assistance; it provides an invaluable partnership with a movement dedicated to empowering the next generation of African leaders.In appreciation of our sponsors’ commitment, we will ensure that each contribution is recognized meaningfully, providing opportunities for exposure, gratitude, and connection with our audience. These exclusive updates will also be shared with sponsors throughout our preparation journey, offering glimpses into our training sessions, interviews with team members, and behind-the-scenes moments that illustrate the hard work, dedication, and excitement leading up to Orate Africa 2024. By sponsoring the Oratory League, supporters join us on a journey toward a brighter future for African youth, standing beside us as we take Botswana’s voice to a continental stage. Each contribution, whether large or small, is deeply appreciated, and our commitment to recognizing sponsors reflects the gratitude we feel for every step made possible through their generosity.
