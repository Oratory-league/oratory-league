Welcome to the Oratory League

Welcome to the Oratory League, a passionate coalition of young, visionary orators from across the best institutions in Botswana. United by our commitment to debate, public speaking, and a shared vision for African unity, we strive to elevate youth voices on topics that shape our world. With resilience and dedication, we prepare to represent Botswana on the continental stage at Orate Africa 2024, bringing stories, perspectives, and dreams that transcend borders. Join us in this journey of eloquence, unity, and impact, as we shoot for the stars for international influence.

Leatile King Baaitse, The President
